Biography

Sridevi Kapoor was born in Sivakasi, India on August 13, 1963. Leading Bollywood actress who earned her 9th Filmfare Best Actress Award nomination for her role in the 1997 film Judaii. In 1998, she won a Filmfare Special Award for her role in Nagina and Mr. India. She co-starred in the musical Lamhe with Anil Kapoor in 1991. She acted in her first Tamil film at age four and won a Best Child Artist Award in 1967.
