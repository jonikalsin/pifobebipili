Sal Governale

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Tuesday, August 27, 2024

Sal Governale is a New York-based actor and producer who is a regular on the Howard Stern Show.

The New York native, along with the closest friend Richard Cristy, carved out a niche with a section dubbed “Phony Phone Calls.”

Howard Stern once praised the duo for reviving his radio show. Fans, unsurprisingly, feel the same way.

Sal also has a YouTube channel called “Big Meals, Small Places with Sal Governale” that he runs in addition to his radio show. His YouTube account, which he launched in August 2013, now has over 2 million views and over 19K subscribers.

Salary & Net Worth of Sal Governale

Sal Governale, like any other New York high schooler, enjoyed rapping and break dancing as a hobby. He enrolled in a community college to pursue a career as a radiologist but eventually dropped out.

Sal subsequently supplemented his income by taking odd jobs before pursuing a profession as a stockbroker. While still working in the corporate sector, he discovered his true calling as the prankster on the Howard Stern Show.

Prior to joining the crew, the father of three was a frequent caller on the show. Fans dubbed him “Sal the Stockbroker.” He finally got recruited as a writer/prank caller after finishing second in the “Win John’s Job” contest.

Sal now reportedly makes more than $80,000 a year as the Howard Stern Show’s producer/senior writer. His affiliation with the nationally syndicated radio show contributes significantly to his $400 thousand net worth.

Sal and his wife are married.

Sal and Richard are accused of being a secret gay couple due to their evident chemistry. In reality, the former could not be more direct.

Sal is a married man with three children. In 1999, he and his wife Christine Governale welcomed their first child. The couple’s middle kid arrived two years later. When Christine gave birth to baby number three in 2003, the family grew to a team of five.

Sal married the mother of his three children in 1996. The previous lives of the married couple are unknown.

His wife, like him, has a successful career. Christine works for Update Staffing Inc. as a Placement Consultant. In 1996, she joined the New York-based law practice.

Age, height, and father’s name

Sal was born on October 24, 1968, as a Muslim on Long Island, New York. His biological parents, a kebab shop owner father and a housewife mother, were of North African ancestry.

The Howard Stern Show host converted to Catholicism as a teenager. He admitted that he couldn’t combat the stigma that comes with being a Muslim in America.

In terms of height, the 50-year-old stands at five feet and ten inches (1.78 m).
