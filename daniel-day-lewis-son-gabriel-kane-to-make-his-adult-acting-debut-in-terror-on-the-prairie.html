Daniel Day-Lewis' Son Gabriel-Kane to Make His Adult Acting Debut in 'Terror on the Prairie'

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, July 8, 2024

Gabriel-Kane Day-Lewis is set to make his adult acting debut!

The 26-year-old model and musician, who is the son of actors Daniel Day-Lewis and Isabelle Adjani, is set to appear in the upcoming western Terror on the Prairie.

Click inside to find out more about the film…

Gabriel-Kane will play the supporting role of The Kid, an outlaw who seems innocent but hides a darker truth.

This will be his first role in a movie since his appearance as a child in Benoît Jacquot’s 2002 French-language drama Adolphe.

The film centers on a pioneering family that fights against a gang of outlaws terrorizing them on their newly built farm and stars former Mandalorian star Gina Carano, who is also producing.
