Klinklang (Japanese: ギギギアル Gigigiaru) is a Metal-type Stage 2 Pokémon card. It is part of the Emerging Powers expansion.

Card text

Charge Beam
チャージビーム		30
Attach an Energy card from your discard pile to this Pokémon.
Zap Cannon
でんじほう		80
Flip a coin. If tails, this Pokémon can't use Zap Cannon during your next turn.

Release information

This card was included in the English Emerging Powers expansion. In Japan, it was released in the Movie Commemoration Value Pack, a promotional item available at 7-11's and Pokémon Centers on July 15, 2011, featuring an exclusive deck box, a Red Collection booster pack, and a booster pack from either White Collection or Black Collection. It was later re-released as one of four cards given out at random starting December 25, 2012 at 7 Eleven stores in Japan to those who purchased at least four booster packs of Dragon Blast, Dragon Blade, Freeze Bolt, Cold Flare, Plasma Gale, Spiral Force, or Thunder Knuckle.

Trivia

Origin

Charge Beam and Zap Cannon are both moves in the Pokémon games that Klinklang can learn. This card's Pokédex entry comes from Pokémon White.
