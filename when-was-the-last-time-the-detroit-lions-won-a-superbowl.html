When was the Last Time the Detroit Lions Won a Superbowl?

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Thursday, June 20, 2024

The Detroit Lions, an iconic team in the NFL, have a rich history, but they have never won a Super Bowl. Their last NFL championship victory came before the AFL-NFL merger in 1970. Here are the details about their last NFL championship wins and their performance in the postseason since then:

Detroit Lions NFL Championship Wins

The Detroit Lions have won the NFL championship four times before the 1970 AFL-NFL merger. They claimed victory in the following years:

  • In 1935, they won their first NFL championship by defeating the New York Giants with a score of 26-7 at the University of Detroit Stadium in Detroit, Michigan.
  • In 1952, they secured their second NFL championship by defeating the Cleveland Browns with a score of 17-7 at Cleveland Municipal Stadium in Cleveland, Ohio.
  • In 1953, they won their third NFL championship by defeating the Cleveland Browns with a score of 17-16 at Briggs Stadium in Detroit.
  • Their fourth and last NFL championship victory came in 1957, where they defeated the Cleveland Browns with a score of 59-14 at Briggs Stadium in Detroit.

Detroit Lions Super Bowl Appearances

The Detroit Lions have never reached the Super Bowl. They are one of the 12 teams in the NFL that have never won a Super Bowl and one of the four teams that have never played in one. Since their last NFL championship victory in 1957, the Lions have not won any championship until now.

Postseason Performance

The Detroit Lions have a playoff record of 7 wins and 13 losses. Their last playoff victory was in 1991, when they defeated the Dallas Cowboys in the Divisional Round. Since the 1970 merger, the Lions have only managed to secure one playoff win. They last made the playoffs in 2016, and the team lost eight straight playoff games in the Wild Card round.

Lions' Recent Performance
