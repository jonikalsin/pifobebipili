OmarGoshTV is a popular American YouTube star known for his channel on which he posts comedy and adventure videos. OmarGoshTV also uploads a variety of content related to horror and haunted houses.

Early Life and Childhood

OmarGoshTV was born on 14 March 1982, in his birthplace, Tampa, Florida, USA. He studied videography and filmmaking at the University of South Florida. His age is 41 years old and his zodiac sign is Pisces. Omar has not shared his parents’ names and any other information related to his family background is unknown.

Professional Life

Even though he studied videography and film-making at the university, OmarGoshTV started with a career in hairdressing. And then, he moved to social media and became a full-time YouTuber to channel his passion. He formed his first YouTube channel OmarGoshTV on November 28, 2011. He uploads videos of pranks and scary adventures every Wednesday and Saturday.

Omar is known for exploring haunted locations at night, which he claims people would not dare to visit even during the day. Some of the haunted locations he has visited are Waverly Hills Sanatorium, Eastern State Penitentiary, Queen Mary Spook, and Hill Biltmore Hotel.

Some of his most popular YouTube videos are ‘NOT HAVING ENOUGH MONEY EXPERIMENT – FAITH IN HUMANITY RESTORED | OmarGoshTV’, ‘HAUNTED CLOWN TUNNEL (GOING ALL THE WAY NEW HAUNTED TUNNEL) | OmarGoshTV’, ‘HAUNTED QUEEN MARY SHIP AT 3 AM – Ghost Hunting In A Haunted Ship! | OmarGoshTV’, ‘THE CHICKEN WAS STILL ALIVE! (FULL VIDEO) | OmarGoshTV’, ‘Real Monster Living In My Pool’, ‘THIS HERO WILL MAKE YOU SMILE – FULL VIDEO as seen on Facebook | OmarGoshTV’, and many more.

Aside from his main YouTube channel, he also has another vlog channel called ‘The Omar Gosh’. The channel is Omar’s daily ‘Life Movies’ channel i.e. vlogs filled with Good vibes, weirdness, funny interactions, random adventures, and much more. It contains videos of him doing random things, and vlogs featuring his crazy family, which he uploads every day. It has many videos that have crossed the million mark, such as ‘Secretly Filming Dog & Cat Shelter’, ‘Embarrassing My Son At School!’, and ‘Finding Money In Car Wash Vacuums.’

Omar collaborated with the famous social media star Moe Sargi, which helped boost his fan following. He has also ventured into entrepreneurship and sells merchandise like T-shirts, hoodies, etc. Omar once did an overnight challenge video at Jake Paul’s Team 10 house.
