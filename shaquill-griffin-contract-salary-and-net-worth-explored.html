Shaquill Griffin contract, salary and net worth explored

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, September 2, 2024

Shaquill Griffin is a famous American footballer born to Tangie and Terry Griffin on July 20, 1995, in St. Petersburg Florida. He is the twin brother of Shaquem Griffin – former linebacker Shaquem Griffin. He was born less than two minutes before Shaquem. He is a talented footballer who plays cornerback for the Jacksonville Jaguars of the National Football League. The Seattle Seahawks selected him in the third round (90th overall) of the 2017 NFL Draft. He was the 13th cornerback selected in 2017.

In this article, we will dive into his career, especially his contract with his current team to know the contract details. Continue reading to know his contract details with the tram – contract duration, worth, annual salary as well as his overall net worth as of October 2022.

Contract

He was drafted by the Seahawks in the 2017 NFL Draft. In 2021, he was traded to the Jaguars, where he currently plays.

On March 17, 2021, Griffin signed a three-year contract with the Jacksonville Jaguars worth $44.5 million, including a $12,000,000 signing bonus and $29,000,000 guaranteed.
