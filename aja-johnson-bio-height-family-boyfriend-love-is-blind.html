If you’ve watched the first season then you are already familiar with the premise of the show if not, then male and female contestants who joined the show work on trying to form the bond without seeing each other. Before the premiere of the show, Aja took to her IG to announce, “ready to see season 2 of @loveisblindnetflix? you might see a familiar face… season premiere February 11th!”. She shared on the show bio that she “can’t stand a bad communicator.”

Aja had been hopeful about finding love on the show but sadly that reportedly didn’t happen for her. There were only two couples who are still together from the show Nick & Danielle and Abhishek ‘Shake’ & Deepti.

Aja Johnson Job

Aja Johnson currently has a job as a paralegal. She debuted her career in September 2015 when she landed the job of Legal Assistant at the 13th Judicial Circuit of Missouri. She continued working there until July 2016 and not long after she joined Korshak Kracoff Kong & Sugano as Temporary Legal Secretary.

Her job in the company only lasted for two months. Then, Aja started working for Neal, Gerber & Eisenberg LLP as a Temporary Receptionist/Legal Assistant. She worked there till June 2017. Later on, she landed the job as Paralegal at Cirignani Heller & Harman, LLP.

Working for seven months, Aja then left the LLP to pursue a Temporary Legal Assistant job at Schwartz Gilligan Ltd. For the next seven months, she worked for the company before joining Katten Muchin Rosenman LLP as Project Assistant. The job continued till July 2020.

Beginning from July 2020, Aja has been working as a paralegal for Blue River PetCare. If you’re unaware of the term and responsibilities, a Paralegal assists with case planning, development, and management, legal research, interviews clients, gather facts and retrieves information, drafts and analyzes legal documents, and collects, complies, and utilizes technical information, to make recommendations to an attorney.

Besides her job, Aja is also a runway fashion model. She modeled for fashion designer Eli Cashh in November 2019. In addition, she also got featured in Jumpman’s Heritage Collection.
