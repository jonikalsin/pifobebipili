Maria Sakkari is a young and talented professional tennis player hailing from Greece. Maria Sakkari recorded her career-high singles ranking of world #3 on 21 March 2022. This made Sakkari the highest-ranked female Greek player in the Open Era.

Early Life

Maria Sakkari was born on 25 July 1995 and she is currently 28 years old. She hails from Athens, Greece, and her zodiac sign is Leo. Her name is written in Greek as Μαρία Σάκκαρη.

Career and Professional Life

Professionally, Maria Sakkari is a young tennis player. This talented tennis professional reached a career-high singles ranking of world #3 on 21 March 2022.

This made Sakkari the highest-ever ranked female Greek player in the Open Era. Likewise, tennis player Sakkari’s best WTA doubles ranking is 169. She achieved this record on 9 September 2019.

Moreover, this player has won one singles title on the WTA Tour. It was back at the 2019 Morocco Open. At this event, Sakkari defeated Johanna Konta in the final.

Likewise, Sakkari was a semifinalist at the 2017 Wuhan Open and she defeated Caroline Wozniacki en route to her 1st top-10 win.

Similarly, in the year 2019, Sakkari reached another Premier 5 semifinal at the Italian Open.

At this event, this player defeated Petra Kvitová among other well-known players. Also, in 2020, this young tennis player reached the 4th round at both the Australian Open and US Open.

Then in 2021, Sakkari reached the semifinals at the French Open as well as the 2021 US Open. This made Sakkari the 1st woman from Greece to reach a Grand Slam semifinal.

More Details

People recognize this player for her aggressive, all-court style of play. Sakkari’s style of playing is centered around her strong serve as well as powerful groundstrokes.

This player served the 6th most aces of any WTA player in 2020. Furthermore, this young player had her debut for the Greece Fed Cup team in 2012. Likewise, Sakkari has a Fed Cup win-loss record of 12–18 to date.

Talking about the beginning, her parents introduced Sakkari to tennis at the young age of 6 years. Subsequently, Sakkari moved to Barcelona at the young age of 18 years for her training.

Sakkari’s favorite surfaces are hard and clay. Additionally, this young player’s favorite shot is the serve. Growing up, Sakkari’s favorite players were Serena Williams, Roger Federer, and Rafael Nadal.

Talking about her coaches, Thomas Johansson coached Sakkari from 2017 to 2018, Mark Petchey in 2018, and Tom Hill is her coach from 2018 to the present (2022).

Furthermore, Sakkari became the best female Greek athlete of the year 2021.
