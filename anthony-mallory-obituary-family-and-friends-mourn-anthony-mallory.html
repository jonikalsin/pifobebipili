Anthony Mallory Obituary, Death – With heavy hearts, we come together to remember and honor the life of Anthony Eugene Mallory, who tragically lost his life in a fatal crash on Interstate 80 in Mercer County.

Anthony Eugene Mallory, 34 years old, hailing from Rochester, New York, was a beloved individual whose life was cut short due to a multi-vehicle crash. His passing leaves a void that will be deeply felt by all who had the privilege of knowing him.

The crash occurred on Interstate 80 at the 14-mile marker, and it involved a tragic pile-up of 8 to 10 vehicles. Anthony was a passenger in one of these vehicles during this unfortunate incident.

Our thoughts and deepest condolences go out to Anthony’s family and friends during this time of immense grief. We stand in solidarity with them as they navigate through the aftermath of this tragic event.

The road remains closed from the Interstate 376 eastbound interchange to Exit 15 (Route 19, Mercer), and as of now, there is no estimated time for the road to reopen. The impact of this accident has also resulted in multiple individuals being transported to the hospital with minor injuries.
