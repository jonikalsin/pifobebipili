Katie Lowes is an American actress, voice actress, and theater director best known for portraying the role of Quinn Perkins in the ABC’s political drama series, Scandal. She has also made short appearances in shows like The Sopranos, Without a Trace, NCIS, Ghost Whisperer, Castle, Leverage, The Closer, Grey’s Anatomy, and Private Practice.
