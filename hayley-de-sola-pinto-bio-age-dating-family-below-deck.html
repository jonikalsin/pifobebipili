Meet Below Deck Hayley De Sola Pinto! Her Age, Dating, Family

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, September 8, 2024

Hayley De Sola Pinto is the new stew who will be working under Captain lee on Season 10 of Below Deck. Excited to be sailing Caribbean waters, Hayley has been working on and off yachts since she was 23 years old.

Learn about Hayley’s Below Deck stint, her net worth, dating life, age, family, and much more in this wiki article.

Hayley De Sola Pinto On Below Deck

Hayley De Sola Pinto first announced that she was going to be part of Below Deck Season 10 via Instagram on October 15, 2022. She shared her official picture from the show in a proper attire of stew and wrote, “Secrets out. Catch me on the biggest and best season yet of @belowdeckbravo. Get ready for big drama, big laughs, and of course BIG HAIR !! Premieres Nov 21st on @bravotv.”

Hayley’s love for travel and meeting new people has led her to start working on yachts on and off. It is the sport that she’s been doing since she turned 23.

Having sailed the shores of Vancouver, Malta, New York, Beverly Hills, and more, it’s quite clear that yachting is one of Hayley’s biggest passions.

In the show Below Deck, she will be sailing the Caribbean waters as a stew while working under Captain Lee.

As per Hayley’s bio on Bravo’s official site, Hayley specialized in making guests feel welcome and at home on St. David with her humor and empathetic personality. However, sometimes her loud and over-the-top personality gets her into the mix of drama onboard.

Hayley De Sola Pinto Net Worth

When Hayley is away from the sea, she works as an Advanced Aesthetic Practitioner. She opened her own beauty firm, Classen Aesthetics, in 2020. Since then, she has been providing services like face contouring, anti-wrinkle injections, and lip fillers through her business. To hone her skills further, Hayley took several masterclasses in October 2022.

As per her business’ website, Hayley prides herself in “having the unique ability to tailor treatments to each individual by both enhancing and complimenting their natural beauty.”

In case you did not know, Hayley is a part of the modeling industry as well and often shares pictures from her modeling shoots on Instagram.

Interestingly, the Below Deck star had the honor of walking the New York City Fashion Week runway for its Couture theme in September 2019. Expressing her gratefulness, she shared, “Just this weekend I was lucky enough to walk the runway at one of the biggest fashion events in the world…  I am forever grateful to be a part of it,” on her social media.

At the event, Hayley’s hair was covered in bouncy curls. Nagwa’s Boutique was the mind behind her breathtaking look and Teofany Manus was the artist behind her make-up.

Coming to Hayley’s net worth, she makes a lot of money from her clinic and modeling business. Also, now that she’s in Below Deck, she also makes a certain amount of money from the show.

While we don’t know Hayley’s exact salary, the second and third stews make $5,000 a month and up to $50,00 at the end of the charter season. The chief stew makes about $5,500 to $6,000 a month and up to $75,000 per charter season.

Hayley’s net worth was estimated to be around $600 thousand as of November 2022.
